Celebrate the
start of Sibos 2012

Bottomline Technologies’ Sunday
Before Sibos events will provide you
exclusive access to some of the best
experiences that Osaka has to offer.
Please join us on Sunday, October
28 and meet Japan with your
friends from Bottomline.

Appreciate
Japanese Traditions

Lunch at one of Osaka’s premier
restaurants, cruise down the Osaka
River and peruse the city’s historical
museums at the ancient Osaka
Castle. Participate at the end of
the tour in an authentic
tea ceremony.

Enjoy the View

Dine Sunday at the towering
Hotel Monterey Grasmere and enjoy
sweeping views of Osaka. Listen to
the flowing notes of a traditional
Japanese Harp (Koto) and see a
Hiragana artist demonstrate script
writing during an
elegant meal.
